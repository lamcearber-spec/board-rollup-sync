Board Rollup Sync Support

Need help with installation, board IDs, column mappings, or marketplace review? Contact support@konverter-pro.de.

What To Include

  • Your monday.com account URL.
  • The source board IDs and target rollup board ID.
  • The column mapping lines you configured.
  • A screenshot of the preview or sync result if the issue is visual.

Review Response Time

Marketplace review and support requests are monitored during German business days. We aim to respond promptly to monday.com review team questions.
